Meet the owner

Hossang opened the first Shish Kabob Cafe in
Houston in 1994.

Since then he has been serving up the most flavorful mediterranean food using the freshest ingredients sourced locally.
Come on by and visit the restaurant that has been a part of Houston's community for several decades!

Open Everyday from 11:00 am to 9:30 pm

Closed on Sunday
